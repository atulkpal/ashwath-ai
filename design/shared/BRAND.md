# Ashwath AI Brand Identity

**Status:** Draft

---

## Product Vision

Ashwath AI is a **local AI workstation**, not a chatbot. It gives developers and power users direct control over private, offline artificial intelligence running on their own hardware. The platform bundles model management, knowledge retrieval, voice, and vision into a single tool that respects user sovereignty.

The brand stands for:
- **Privacy:** All inference happens locally. No telemetry, no cloud dependency.
- **Capability:** Professional-grade tools that unlock the full potential of on-device AI.
- **Control:** Users own their hardware, their models, and their data. The interface reflects that ownership.

---

## Brand Name

- **Full name:** Ashwath AI
- **Always capitalize:** A in "Ashwath", A and I in "AI"
- **Never:** ashwath, ASHWATH

---

## Tagline

> Offline-first AI platform. Private. Modular. Cross-platform.

---

## Brand Personality

The Ashwath AI brand is:

| Trait | Manifestation |
|-------|---------------|
| **Precise** | Every pixel serves a purpose. No decoration. |
| **Authoritative** | Speaks with confidence. Technical and direct. |
| **Private** | Offline-first by default. Zero telemetry. User sovereignty. |
| **Developer-centric** | Tools over toys. Power over simplicity. |
| **Futuristic** | Cold, precise, synthetic. Not warm or playful. |

---

## Voice and Tone

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Error messages | Technical but helpful. State what happened and how to resolve. |
| Status updates | Direct and minimal. Use monospace for technical readouts. |
| Onboarding | Confident and reassuring. Emphasize privacy and control. |
| Marketing | Aspirational but grounded. Focus on capability, not hype. |

**Never:**
- Use emoji in technical contexts
- Use exclamation marks in UI copy
- Address the user as "friend" or similar
- Make promises about future capabilities

---

## Colour DNA

- **Primary:** `#00f0ff` (Cyan) — The Glow. Used for interaction and AI states only.
- **Background:** `#000000` — The Void. Pure black for maximum contrast.
- **Surfaces:** `#121212`, `#0c0c0c`, `#1a1a1a` — Tonal greys for depth.
- **Borders:** `#27272a` — Subtle hairlines defining structure.
- **Text:** `#ffffff` (primary), `#a1a1a1` (secondary) — High contrast hierarchy.

---

## Logo and Icon

- The Terminal icon (`>` style) is the primary app icon.
- The icon should always be rendered in cyan (`#00f0ff`) on dark backgrounds.
- No wordmark logo exists for this version. The brand name is set in Inter Semi Bold.
